Let's not talk for a while
just lie here and watch

It's lovely
Twilight settles
a feeling of peace

Swallows pass on silent wings
animals sniff the familiar
hear the trill of the tree toad
the occasional sounds comfort

he loved life
loved to be a part of the world

The thought of death came

"I don't want to die."
"Of course you don't."

"I love it here. I love everything."
"Of course you do. We all do."
